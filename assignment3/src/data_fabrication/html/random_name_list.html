There are a large number of reasons you may need to create a random name. Below you'll find some common reasons people use this generator.

Character Names

Those writing novels or other books may need to come up with various character names. Coming up with the perfect name for a minor or secondary character can often be difficult. Creating random names is a great way to brainstorm and see names to helpt the author find the perfect name for each character.

Make Things Easier

If you have a name that you don't like, or you come from a foreign country where your given name is difficult for English speakers to pronounce, coming up with an alternative name to go by makes a lot of sense. Doing makes life easier on yourself and those around you.

Website Signups

It's common to have to sign up with websites these days, but there will be time when you want to sign up, but you don't necessarily want to give your real name in the process. Coming up with a new name to use for these purposes is a way to solve this problem.

Online Activity

There are many people who are online who wish to keep their identity secret for a variety of legitimate reasons. For those who don't use their real name, finding a random name is important so they can remain anonymous.

Pen Names

There may be times when you don't want to use your real name when writing an article, book or column. There are a lot of people who write under pen names. This tool can help to come up with that perfect pen name.

Baby Names

Looking through pages and pages of baby names can be quite tiring and frustrating. For soon-to-be parents, it's sometimes more effective to have random names appear which can give them an split second to react to whether or not the name is one they may want to use.

Pet Names

For those who are looking to come up with a unique name for their pet, going through a list of random names can be a wonderful way to brainstorm t ultimately find the perfect name for the pet.
